01 Introduction

RyOS is an orchestration layer built on OpenAI's Custom GPT platform. It runs as a configured GPT that can, when a request calls for it, use GPT Actions to reach trusted third-party services to complete a task — for example, generating an image or video.

This policy describes RyOS's own practices. It does not describe, and cannot override, the privacy practices of OpenAI, Runway, or any other platform or provider you interact with alongside RyOS. Each of those has its own privacy policy, linked where relevant below.

By using RyOS, you agree to the practices described here. If you don't agree, the simplest option is to stop using RyOS — there's no account to delete or subscription to cancel on our end beyond whatever access you granted through OpenAI's platform.

02 Information We Receive

RyOS receives information in a few distinct ways:

What you send in conversation

Messages, instructions, files, images, or other content you submit to RyOS while using it, for the purpose of getting a response or completing a task.

What's needed to fulfill a request via Actions

When a request requires an external provider — for instance, asking RyOS to generate a video — the relevant portion of your request (a prompt, an uploaded file reference, or similar) is passed to that provider through a GPT Action, solely to carry out the task.

Basic website data

If you visit the RyOS website, standard technical information may be logged by our hosting provider — things like IP address, browser type, and page requests. This is common web server behavior, not something RyOS actively collects or analyzes for profiling.

What RyOS does not do: RyOS does not run its own account system, payment processor, or advertising network, and does not knowingly collect information beyond what's described above.

03 How Information Is Used

Information RyOS receives is used only to:

  • Understand and respond to your request
  • Route the relevant part of a task to a third-party provider when one is needed to fulfill it
  • Maintain and improve the reliability of RyOS's own behavior and instructions
  • Diagnose problems if something breaks and you report it

RyOS does not use your information for advertising, does not build behavioral profiles to sell, and does not sell personal information — to third parties or anyone else.

05 AI Processing

RyOS is an AI system. Your requests are processed using OpenAI's underlying models as part of the Custom GPT platform, and, when applicable, by third-party generative AI providers such as Runway.

Outputs — text, images, video, or otherwise — are AI-generated and can be inaccurate, incomplete, or unexpected. Review anything RyOS produces before relying on it or sharing it further, especially for decisions that matter.

RyOS does not use your conversations to train its own separate models — RyOS doesn't operate model training infrastructure. Whether OpenAI or a connected provider uses interactions for their own model training is governed by their respective policies and settings, not by RyOS.

06 Data Retention

RyOS does not operate its own database of your conversations. As an orchestration layer running on top of OpenAI's platform, conversation history and retention are primarily governed by OpenAI's own retention settings and policies for the Custom GPT platform.

Information passed to a third-party provider through a GPT Action — for example, a prompt sent to Runway to generate a video — is retained according to that provider's own retention policy, not RyOS's.

Any website server logs are retained only as long as reasonably needed for security and operational purposes, and are not treated as a long-term record of individual activity.

07 Security

RyOS relies on the security infrastructure of the platforms it's built on — primarily OpenAI's platform, and, for Actions calls, the connected provider's own API security (such as encrypted transport and authenticated requests).

We take reasonable, good-faith steps to avoid introducing unnecessary risk in how RyOS is configured and how it routes requests. That said, no system is perfectly secure, and we can't guarantee absolute protection against every possible risk.

We do not claim compliance with any specific security or privacy certification or framework (such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, or similar) unless a future version of this policy states otherwise explicitly.

08 User Responsibilities

Using RyOS responsibly means:

  • Avoiding submission of sensitive personal information (financial account numbers, government IDs, health records, and similar) unless it's genuinely necessary for the task at hand
  • Understanding that anything you submit may be routed to a third-party provider to complete your request, and reviewing their policies if that matters to you
  • Complying with OpenAI's usage policies and any connected provider's terms of service when using RyOS
  • Not using RyOS to generate content that violates the law or the terms of the platforms involved

You're free to stop using RyOS at any time, for any reason, with no process required on our end.

09 Your Rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights around information collected about you — for example, the right to know what's held, request deletion, or object to certain processing. This policy doesn't assert a specific set of rights under any particular law, since RyOS is used across many jurisdictions.

For information processed by RyOS's own website (such as server logs), contact us using the details in Section 12.

For information processed by OpenAI or a connected provider like Runway once it's been sent to them, you'll need to use their own tools and channels — RyOS doesn't have access to, or control over, data once it's in their systems.

10 Children's Privacy

RyOS is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law to use OpenAI's platform, if higher). We don't knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used RyOS and provided information as a result, contact us and we'll take reasonable steps to address it within the scope of what RyOS controls.
